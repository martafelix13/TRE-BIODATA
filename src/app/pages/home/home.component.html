What is a Trusted Research Environment?

A Trusted Research Environment (TRE) is a secure, controlled platform for researchers to analyze sensitive data in compliance with privacy and security standards.

Developed in Portugal

This TRE was developed by Biodata.pt, ensuring compliance with national and European data security guidelines.

Built on the Five Safes Framework

This TRE follows the Five Safes principles to protect sensitive data access. It is also part of the European EOSC-ENTRUST initiative.
