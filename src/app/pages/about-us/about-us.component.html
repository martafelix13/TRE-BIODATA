About ADELE

Advanced Data Environment for Linked Evidence

ADELE is a secure and scalable Trusted Research Environment (TRE) developed in collaboration with INESC, enabling ethical research using sensitive health and administrative data. Built upon the SATRE architecture, ADELE prioritizes compliance, security, and scientific value.

The Five Safes Framework

Safe People

Access is restricted to trained, accredited researchers following ethical review.

Safe Projects

Projects undergo rigorous approval processes to ensure scientific value and compliance.

Safe Data

Data is de-identified and minimized to reduce risk while supporting meaningful analysis.

Safe Settings

Research is performed in a monitored, encrypted virtual environment with strict controls.

Safe Outputs

All outputs are manually checked for disclosure risk before release.

Technical Foundations

ADELE is based on the SATRE framework, ensuring modular, policy-driven architecture with features like network segmentation, reproducible configurations, and audit-ready logging.

Handling Sensitive Health Data

  • Data Storage: AES-256 encrypted storage with role-based access control and redundancy.
  • Data Access: Accessed only through secure, monitored virtual desktops with audit logging.
  • Data Management: Custodians oversee lifecycle, ingestion, and metadata tracking with full transparency.
